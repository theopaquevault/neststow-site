(5) Special Instructions for the Reminder Function

The expiration date reminder function of this APP relies on the local notification mechanism of the iOS system. Due to system limitations, this APP can schedule up to 64 reminder notifications to be triggered simultaneously. When the number of items you add exceeds this limit, the reminder for the earliest expiring item may be automatically overwritten by the system and fail to trigger.

The punctuality of reminders is affected by various factors such as device status, system settings, network environment, and phone permissions. We cannot guarantee that reminders will be triggered 100% on time. For items that may affect health, such as food and medicine, please do not rely solely on the reminder function of this APP. We recommend that you also use system calendars, alarms, and other methods for cross-verification.

This APP and its developer shall not assume any legal liability for any losses caused by failure to enable notification permissions, adding items exceeding the system limit, relying solely on this APP's reminders as the sole basis for judgment, etc.
